SCRUM Days

Each group member should give a brief intro to the group explaining:

  1. What progress have you made?
  2. What obstacles have you encountered?
  3. What have you tried, where have you looked, ask for help?
  4. What are your plans for further development of your site?
  5. What questions do you have for the team about your site?

Each of the other group members should provide feedback detailing how they think their fellow group member should go about the work of further developing their portfolio
